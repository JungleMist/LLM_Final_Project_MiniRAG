# Week 7 Overview

This week explores critical ideas for choosing and evaluating LLMs.  In an ideal world, one might want the most highly performant LLM model, but in a more practical sense, one also needs LLM models that fit in memory, respond quickly, and are affordable to run.  This week starts us off on these considerations by looking at model capacity, evaluation, and how we might quantitatively say that some LLMs are better than others (or better for us to use).
