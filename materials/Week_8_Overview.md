# Week 8 Overview

This week explores critical methodologies for optimization and approximation that aim to alleviate the massive computational demands of LLM use.  In an ideal world, one might want the most highly performant LLM model, but in a more practical sense, one also needs LLM models that fit in memory, respond quickly, and are affordable to run.  This week's topics look at strategies for managing these trade-offs.
