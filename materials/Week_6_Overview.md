# Week 6 Overview

This week covers agentic LLMs.  Agentic LLM systems are AI models that don’t just generate text on demand, but can autonomously decide what to do next—setting sub-goals, calling tools, retrieving information, and iterating toward an objective. Instead of being a passive “autocomplete,” they behave more like software agents that plan, act, and reflect in order to accomplish complex tasks with minimal human micromanagement.
